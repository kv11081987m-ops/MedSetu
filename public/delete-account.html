Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can request permanent deletion of your MedSetu account and all associated data by following the steps below.

⚠️ Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your order history, saved addresses, and personal data will be permanently removed.

What will be deleted

  • Your profile (name, phone number, email)
  • Saved delivery addresses
  • Order history and medicine records
  • All account preferences and settings

How to request account deletion

1

Send an email to support@medsetu.app with subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

2

Include your registered mobile number or email address in the email body.

3

We will process your request within 7 working days and send a confirmation.
